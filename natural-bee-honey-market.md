<p><p>The natural bee honey market is segmented into two main types: polyfloral and monofloral honey. Polyfloral honey is sourced from the nectar of various flowers, resulting in a diverse flavor profile and potential health benefits. It typically varies with seasons and locations. In contrast, monofloral honey is derived from a single flower species, offering specific tastes and qualities linked to that flower. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and are valued for their unique characteristics and potential health advantages.</p></p>
