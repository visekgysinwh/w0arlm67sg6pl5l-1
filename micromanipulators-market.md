<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Micromanipulator</li><li>Electric Micromanipulator</li><li>Manual Micromanipulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micromanipulator market is categorized into three main types: Hydraulic, Electric, and Manual micromanipulators. Hydraulic micromanipulators utilize fluid pressure for precise movements, offering high stability and force, ideal for delicate tasks. Electric micromanipulators rely on electric motors for enhanced control and automation, providing versatility in various applications. Manual micromanipulators are operated by hand, offering simplicity and direct control, often preferred for straightforward tasks. Each type serves distinct needs across fields like biomedical research, microelectronics, and materials science.</p></p>
