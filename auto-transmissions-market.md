<p><p>The auto transmission market encompasses various types tailored for vehicle performance and efficiency. Manual Transmission (MT) offers driver control through gear shifting. Automatic Transmission (AT) simplifies operation by automatically changing gears. Automated Manual Transmission (AMT) combines elements of both, offering automated gear shifts while retaining a manual system's efficiency. Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) provides a smooth driving experience with variable gear ratios. Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT) enhances performance by using two clutches for quick, efficient gear changes, merging manual and automatic elements.</p></p>
