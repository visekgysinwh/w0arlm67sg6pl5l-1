<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Uncooled Thermal Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave IR</li><li>Mid Wave IR</li><li>Long Wave IR</li><li>Far Wave IR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled thermal imaging market is categorized into four types based on infrared wavelengths. Short Wave IR (SWIR) detects thermal radiation in the 0.9-1.7 micrometer range, ideal for applications like inspection and sensing. Mid Wave IR (MWIR) operates from 3-5 micrometers, used in military and industrial applications. Long Wave IR (LWIR), ranging from 8-14 micrometers, is widely used for surveillance and building inspections. Far Wave IR, extending beyond 14 micrometers, is less common, primarily utilized in scientific research and specialized applications.</p></p>
