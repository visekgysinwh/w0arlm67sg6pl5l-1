<p><p>The Food Grade Gellan Gum market is categorized into two main types: High Acyl Gellan Gum and Low Acyl Gellan Gum. High Acyl Gellan Gum is characterized by its gelling and thickening properties, ideal for producing elastic gels and enhancing texture in food products. Both types are widely used in the food industry for their versatility and stabilizing properties.</p></p>
