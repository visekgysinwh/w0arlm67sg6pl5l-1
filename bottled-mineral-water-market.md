<p><ul><li>Natural Mineral Water</li><li>Artificial Mineral Water</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bottled mineral water market is divided into two main types: natural mineral water and artificial mineral water. Natural mineral water is sourced from mineral springs and contains naturally occurring minerals, offering unique health benefits and flavors. In contrast, artificial mineral water is manufactured by adding minerals to purified water, allowing for control over mineral content and taste. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, with natural options often perceived as healthier, while artificial variants are typically more affordable and widely available.</p></p>
