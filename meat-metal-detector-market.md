<p><ul><li>Detectors with a General Purpose Head</li><li>Detectors with a Ferrous-In Foil Search Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The meat metal detector market is divided into two main types: detectors with a general-purpose head and detectors with a ferrous-in-foil search head. General-purpose head detectors are versatile and designed for broad applications, effectively identifying metal contaminants across various food products. In contrast, ferrous-in-foil search head detectors are specialized for detecting ferrous metals, even in challenging environments like foil packaging. These distinctions allow food processors to select detectors tailored to their specific contamination detection needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
