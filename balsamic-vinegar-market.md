<p><p>The balsamic vinegar market is primarily divided into two types: Traditional Balsamic and Commercial Balsamic. Traditional Balsamic is a high-quality product from Modena or Reggio Emilia, crafted using a specific aging process with strict regulations, featuring a complex flavor profile. In contrast, Commercial Balsamic is more affordable and widely available, often made from cheaper wine vinegar and caramel color, resulting in a simpler taste.
